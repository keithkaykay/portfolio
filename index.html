Akan name

This application will give you your Akan name. Your Akan name depends on the day of the week you were born and your gender.
It calculates the day of the week you were born from the date of your birth.
Akan names are derived from Ghanian culture. Frequently in Ghana, children are given their first name as a 'day name' which corresponds to the day in the week they were born.
